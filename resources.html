Interactive tools from the Miao Lab for exploring immune gene expression data — no installation required, runs entirely in your browser.

01

Bone Marrow Macrophage Array

Developed by Yaxin “Zoe” Liu

An interactive browser for a genome-wide microarray time-course of primary bone-marrow-derived macrophages (BMDMs) stimulated with a panel of innate-immune ligands and cytokines. Search any gene to explore its expression dynamics across conditions and time points.

Launch Tool → View on GitHub
02

RNA-seq Explorer

Developed by Yaxin “Zoe” Liu

A browser-based tool for exploring bulk RNA-seq differential expression. Runs a complete DESeq2-style workflow — differential testing, PCA, heatmaps, pathway enrichment, and transcription-factor activity — entirely in your browser, with no installation and no server. Upload your count matrix and get publication-ready figures in minutes.
